about me

Professional carpenter turned software developer.

My story is of a woman building foundations. I am a fast and passionate learner. After mastering the trade, I began tackling more business-related roles such as managing jobs, estimating materials and labor costs, and communicating with builders, superintendents, and homeowners. I became a dedicated owner of Gates Construction in January of 2014.

After years of hard physical labor, I decided that a career shift was necessary. After much research, I realized that software development shared many of the skills such as creativity, problem-solving, teamwork, and clear communication with my years of work in construction. Both emphasize designing and delivering a quality end result. Now I’m exchanging my hammer for a keyboard, and I couldn’t be happier.
